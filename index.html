Overview

Experience a 2D, gacha‑style, turn‑based RPG set in the SSIA Universe. Step into the shoes of a fallen agent, mastering elemental powers and strategic combat in Sierra Liudoxoya.

Bellemaire Chapter 1: ”kicks off in the capital, Bellemaire—combining dialogue choices, tactical battles, and elemental exploration.”

  • Seven elemental types: Pyro, Hydro, Electro, Cryro, Geo, Spectro & Void
  • Gacha mechanics drive character and weapon draws
  • Choice-driven dialogue shapes your journey
  • Combat training with Fritz unlocks quests
