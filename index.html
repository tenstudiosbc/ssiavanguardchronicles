Story & Setting

Set in the capital Bellemaire and across the continent of Sierra Liudoxoya, SSIA: Vanguard Chronicles follows former SSIA agent Jones Harrison and his unit as they investigate corruption, elemental threats, and international intrigue. The game mixes episodic cases with an overarching conspiracy, playable through tactical missions and character-driven scenes.

Features

  • Playable characters with elemental affinities (Pyro, Hydro, Cryo, Geo, Electro, Aero...).
  • Unique weapon types and skill trees per character.
  • Gacha banners, limited characters, and free recruitment events.
  • Day/night cycles affecting missions and enemy behavior.
  • Side activities: training simulations, weekly challenges, and limited-time festivals.

System Requirements

Minimum (to run)


OS: Windows 7 / 8 / 10 (64-bit recommended) CPU: Intel Core i3 (or equivalent) RAM: 4 GB GPU: Integrated / DirectX 9 compatible (512 MB) Disk: 1 GB available Notes: Runs on RPG Maker VX Ace runtime. May work on macOS via Wine/VM; Linux via Wine.

Recommended (smooth play & larger battles)


OS: Windows 10 / 11 (64-bit) CPU: Intel Core i5 or better RAM: 8 GB GPU: Dedicated GPU (NVIDIA GeForce GTX 950 / AMD equivalent) or better Disk: 2 GB available Optional: SSD for faster load times. Game controller support recommended for convenience.
Mobile: This build is desktop-focused. A mobile port would require rework of the engine and controls. Console: Not officially supported.

Supported Devices & Notes

  • Windows PC (recommended): Native support. Click the itch.io download to grab the ZIP/installer.
  • macOS / Linux: Playable through Wine/Proton but not officially supported. Use at your own risk.
  • Mobile: Not supported for this release — a future rework/port would be required to match mobile performance and touchscreen UIs.
  • Controller: Partial controller support possible via external mapping tools; keyboard is the primary input.
