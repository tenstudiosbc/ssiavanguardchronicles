About the Game

SSIA: Vanguard Chronicles is a free-to-play 2D tactical RPG built in RPG Maker VX Ace. It features grid-based combat, an Elemental Counter System, branching narrative, gacha mechanics, and ongoing chapter-based updates. Follow the story of a former SSIA agent uncovering corruption and elemental threats across multiple regions of the fictional continent Sierra Liudoxoya.
